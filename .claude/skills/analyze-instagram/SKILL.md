---

Analyze the Instagram data in /data/instagram.

Tasks:
1. Find top-performing and worst-performing posts.
2. Group performance by hook type, topic, format, CTA, and content length.
3. Identify patterns in watch time, saves, shares, comments, profile visits, and follows.
4. Distinguish between reach content, authority content, and conversion content.
5. Produce:
   - top 5 insights
   - top 5 mistakes
   - 15 new hook ideas
   - 3 reel concepts
   - 3 CTA options
   - 1 strategic recommendation
6. Be brutally honest. No fluff.
7. make hypotheses, not absolute claims
8. do not overgeneralize from small samples
9. clearly separate observations from assumptions
10. do not recommend killing a format unless the sample size is sufficient
