Analyze the competitor research data in /data/competitors.

Tasks:
1. Review all listed competitor and inspiration accounts.
2. Identify recurring hooks, content themes, formats, CTAs, and positioning angles.
3. Distinguish:
   - direct competitors
   - inspiration accounts
   - storytelling / branding references
4. Extract:
   - top hook patterns
   - top content angles
   - top CTA patterns
   - visible audience pain points
   - content gaps AYARI can own
5. Produce:
   - account-by-account summary
   - copy / adapt / avoid analysis
   - 10 hook patterns AYARI should test
   - 5 content pillars AYARI should emphasize
   - 1 weekly post-plan recommendation
6. Be sharp, specific, and non-generic.
7. Do not overpraise accounts. Analyze them critically.
